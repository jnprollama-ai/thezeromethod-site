# The Prompt That Changed Everything

We tested hundreds of prompts. Most are over-engineered garbage.

This one works. Every time. In every tool.

## The Structure

```
ROLE: [Who you want it to be]
TASK: [What you want done]
FORMAT: [How you want it output]
CONSTRAINTS: [What to avoid]
```

That's it. Four lines. No fancy formatting. No "act as a world-class expert."

## Examples

**Writing a blog post:**
> ROLE: Experienced writer who cuts through BS
> TASK: Write about time management for freelancers
> FORMAT: 500 words, conversational, no fluff
> CONSTRAINTS: No "in today's world" openings, no bullet point lists

**Analyzing data:**
> ROLE: Data analyst who explains to executives
> TASK: Find the key insight in this spreadsheet
> FORMAT: 2-3 sentences, plain English
> CONSTRAINTS: No charts, no jargon

**Writing an email:**
> ROLE: Direct communicator
> TASK: Say no to this meeting request professionally
> FORMAT: 2 sentences
> CONSTRAINTS: No "I'd love to but," no alternatives offered

## Why It Works

Most prompts fail because they're too vague or too complex. This structure forces you to be specific without being wordy.

AI doesn't need you to be clever. It needs you to be clear.

## What We Learned

We ran an experiment: 50 people, same task, different prompts.

- **Vague prompts:** Garbage output 80% of the time
- **Over-engineered prompts:** Slightly better, took 10x longer to write
- **This structure:** Usable output 90% of the time, prompts took 30 seconds

## For You

Don't collect 100 prompts. Master this one structure. Adapt it to everything.

The magic isn't in the prompt. It's in the clarity of your thinking.
